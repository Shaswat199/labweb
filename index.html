Who We Are

A lab built on curiosity, rigor, and impact

Founded in 2016, NeuralNexus Lab is a research group at the intersection of machine learning, computational neuroscience, and AI systems. We believe the best science happens when diverse minds work on hard problems together.

Our work spans theory to deployment — from mathematical foundations of learning algorithms to real-world systems that solve pressing problems in healthcare, climate, and human cognition.

What We Do

Research Areas

Our lab works across four core tracks, each addressing fundamental challenges in intelligent systems.

Track 01
Deep Learning Theory
Understanding generalization, expressivity, and optimization landscapes of modern neural architectures — moving beyond empirical observations to principled theory.
Optimization Generalization Transformers
Track 02
Computational Neuroscience
Bridging AI and brain science — using machine learning to decode neural data and using neuroscience to inspire better AI algorithms.
fMRI Analysis Neural Coding Brain-AI
Track 03
Robust & Safe AI
Developing systems that are reliable under distribution shift, adversarial conditions, and real-world complexity. Building trustworthy AI from the ground up.
Robustness Safety Uncertainty
Track 04
AI for Science
Applying machine learning to accelerate discovery in genomics, climate modeling, materials science, and drug design — making AI a tool for humanity's hardest problems.
Genomics Climate Drug Discovery
